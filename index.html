The Mystical Taxi Number

The story of the "taxi-cab number" is legendary in mathematics. During a hospital visit, Hardy mentioned arriving in a taxi numbered a somewhat dull number. Ramanujan quickly replied that it was a very interesting number: the smallest number expressible as the sum of two cubes in two distinct ways.
